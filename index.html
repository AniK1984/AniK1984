Stalevo- [sinemet+entacapone]

  • Dosing
    --6 different strengths Stalevo 50, 75, 100, 125, 150 and 200--each form has 200mg entacapone
    --Use in people who have fluctuations/ wearing off
    --max dose of entacapone per day is 1600mg.
    --How to switch? Choose equivalent dose-- For example, patients receiving one tablet of carbidopa/levodopa 25 mg/100 mg and one tablet of entacapone 200 mg at each administration can switch to a single Stalevo 100 tablet (containing 25 mg of carbidopa, 100 mg of levodopa and 200 mg of entacapone)
    --If pt on >600mg levadopa per day, they may need reduction in levadopa dose before adding entacapone.
  • Warnings
    --Dont use along with NON-selective MAO inhibitor, Okay to use with selective MAO-b inhibitors
    --Dont use in Glaucoma or melanoma
    --Caution in people with renal or hepatic dysfunction
    --Caution in people with MI/ cardiac arrythmias

Entacapone--COMT inhibitor

  • Dosing
    --helps with wearing off-- keeps dopamine longer in the system
    --200 mg with each dose of levodopa/carbidopa, up to a maximum of 8 times daily (maximum daily dose: 1600 mg daily)
    --to optimize therapy, the dosage of levodopa may need to be reduced or the dosing interval may need to be extended.
  • Warnings
    --confusion in elederly
    --Avoid using together with non-selective MAO-I, can use with selective MAO-I like selegiline, Rasagiline
    --May enhance side effects of sinemet.

Azilect(Rasagiline)--MAO-b inhibitor

  • Dosing
    --start at 0.5mg daily, can increase to max of 1mg/day
    --can use in young people
    --if you add to levadopa, consider decrease ldopa dose to reduce dyskinesias
    --Use for mild disease or use along with sinemet.
  • Warnings
    --Check for drug interactions--lot of them.
    --basically same side fx as sinemet
    --high risk for serotonin syndrome--DONT use with SSRI/SNRI/TCA
    --Dont use with other MAO inhibitors
    --Dont use with Tramadol
    -- no overlap for 14 days with above meds, if long half life like prozac--no overlap for 5 weeks.
    --Avoid in pts with h/o psychosis-can make it worse.
    --stop before 14 days of surgery with anesthesia or use a MAO safe anesthetic.
    --Dont DC abruptly--it can cause NMS.
    --Dont use with high(>150mg) tyramine foods like cheese
  • Monitoring
    --Monitor Blood pressure)it can raise it.
    --Monitor cbc/cmp.
    --Monitor IOP and skin moles.
